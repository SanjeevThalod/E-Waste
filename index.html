Our NetWork

E-Waste recycling

E-waste recycling is one of the most talked-about issues in the world today due to its potentials to reduce environmental hazards and pollution. E-waste recycling is the reuse and reprocessing of electrical and electronic equipment of any type that has been discarded or regarded as obsolete.

Recycling of e-waste is a growing trend and was initiated to protect human and environmental health mainly due to the widespread environmental pollution impacts of e-waste.

Where everyone saw the problem, we saw the potential. We are tirelessly working on proper disposal of electronic and plastic waste, which is being generated at a rapid pace due to the evolving technology and consumer behavior towards the use of gadgets and devices.

Our semi-automated plants with requisite resources, technology and machinery, authorized by Central Pollution Control Board, makes our process not only sustainable, but also prevents addition of any hazardous impurities to the environment.
